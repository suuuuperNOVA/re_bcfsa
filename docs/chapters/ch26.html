Software

    • WEBForms \(^\circledR\)

  • MLS \(^\circledR\)

  • Budget tracking and accounting

  • Database

  • Photo editing and marketing

Technologies devices contributing to licensee’s competitive advantages¶

  • DSLR camera

  • Drones

    • Drone pilots must carry valid certificates
